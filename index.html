INSTRUCTIONS

Welcome to Space Shooter! Your objective is to survive as long as you can by blasting the enemy out of the sky. But be careful, the longer you survive, the harder it becomes. NOTE: Reading the recent update is recommended! This will inform you of currently-known gameplay-affecting bugs!

Controls

Move Up: W or Up Arrow Key

Move Down: S or Down Arrow Key

Fire: Spacebar

Pause: P

v1.0 Release Notes

    Main Changes:
  • Levels 6-10 have been created. Enemies in these levels will spawn significantly quicker than 1-5, but their speed is slightly slower
  • You can now pause the game by pressing P
    Bug Fixes:
  • No longer able to rapidly fire by holding down spacebar
  • Pressing another button while moving will no longer stop your movement. This includes how firing while moving stopped your player
  • Your shots will no longer go through the enemy
